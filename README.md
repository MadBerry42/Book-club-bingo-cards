# Book-club-bingo-cards
Spice up your book club night with a healthy dose of competitivity! Stick your guesses to the wall and at the end of the book see who guessed the most ;)

Originally conceived as a way to do some sticking-notes-on a wall guessing game for books in a book club, this program can be used for any kind of brainstorming.

In order to have your personal board with your sticky notes you will need to create you own .env file containing your own SUPABASE_URL and SUPABASE_KEY. You can easily do that on the supabase website and then you will be able to deploy your app on Streamlit cloud and share the link with your brainstorming buddies!
